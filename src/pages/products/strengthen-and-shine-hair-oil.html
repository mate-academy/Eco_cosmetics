Strengthen & Shine Hair Oil

660 Uah

30 ml

Ingredients: capryli /capric triglyceride, argania spinosa, ribes nigrum, prunus avium, hippohae rhamnoides, pelargonium roseum, citrus paradise (naturally occurring allergens:, citral, limonene, linalool, geraniol, citronellol)

Application: on damp hair after washing, put a few drops of oil into the palm of your hand, then thoroughly massage on the ends, then blow-dry and style as usual. Perfect for fly-aways too. Start with just a couple drops - a little goes a long way. Reduce amount if you find that your hair is too heavy or greasy-looking after usage. If you have very thick wavy hair you may need to use a little more. If your hair is thin, you will only need one or two drops.
