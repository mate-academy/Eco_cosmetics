about our brand

Our brand cosmetics are made from
natural ingredients and we are promoting them to the masses. Shampoo, hair conditioner, soap - in recycled paper. Creams, oils, washing products in glass containers with recycled paper labels. No synthetics and microplastics in cosmetics. And the packaging from it can be returned for recycling or reused.

My name is Victoria, I'm a master at candle making and natural cosmetics. In the past, a cynologist, was engaged in herbs, made layouts on the Tarot.

I am also a volunteer to save wild birds in distress, a consultant to the Phoenix Wildlife Rehabilitation Center, ab member of the Society for theb Conservation of Birds of Ukraine with the support of Birdlife International.

My husband and I live in the Carpathian region in the city of Chernivtsi. I dream of my center for the rehabilitation of wild birds, as it is very difficult to rescue birds in a rented communal apartment.
