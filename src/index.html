about our brand

Our brand cosmetics are made from natural ingredients and we are promoting them to the masses. Shampoo, hair conditioner, soap - in recycled paper. about the creators

My name is Victoria, I’m a master at candle making and natural cosmetics. In the past, a cynologist, was engaged in herbs, made layouts on the Tarot.

I am also a volunteer to save wild birds in distress, a consultant to the Phoenix Wildlife Rehabilitation Center, a member of the Society for the Conservation of Birds of Ukraine with the support of Birdlife International My husband and I live in the Carpathian region in the city of Chernivtsi. I dream of my center for the rehabilitation of wild birds, as it is very difficult to rescue birds in a rented communal apartment.
